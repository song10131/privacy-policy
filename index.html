Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 26, 2026

Sajeonjeomgeom Bogoseo (Pre-inspection Report) is a construction pre-inspection report service provided by Bandal. We value your privacy and comply with the Korean Personal Information Protection Act.

1. Information We Collect

This app does not require account registration and does not collect or transmit personal information to any server.

The app may access the following device permissions for its functionality:

  • Camera: Used to take photos of defects
  • Photo Library: Used to select photos and save reports/photos
  • Microphone: Used for audio recording during video capture

These permissions are only used when explicitly granted by you and can be changed at any time in your device settings.

2. Purpose of Data Processing

The device permissions accessed are used solely for the following purposes:

  • Taking and selecting photos of construction defects
  • Creating and saving pre-inspection reports (PDF)
  • Storing reports and photos on the device

3. Data Retention

All data generated by this app (photos, reports, etc.) is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any external server. Deleting the app will remove all data stored within it.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share your personal information with any third parties.

5. Data Deletion

All data stored on your device is automatically deleted when the app is uninstalled. You can also delete data directly within the app.

6. Your Rights

You may exercise the following rights at any time:

  • Change camera, photo library, and microphone access permissions in device settings
  • Delete data directly within the app
  • Completely delete all data by uninstalling the app

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in accordance with changes in laws and policies. Any changes will be communicated through app updates.
